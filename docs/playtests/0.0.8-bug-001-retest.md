## Test cases

### Test 1 — Capture clarity and saved party

- Requirement IDs: BUG-001-B, BUG-001-C, BUG-001-D
- Purpose: verify the repaired capture path and the already-observed save behavior without testing installation.
- Starting state: Build 0.0.8 on Windows, existing save, Mossmere Path, Mirelume visible if not previously captured.
- Steps:
  1. If Mirelume is already captured, open Party, confirm both members, refresh, confirm Build 0.0.8, and inspect Party again; then report the capture steps as BLOCKED by the completed save.
  2. Otherwise, approach Mirelume and press E. Confirm five Prisms and the labels **Wild Aetherling** over Mirelume and **Your lead** over Kivren.
  3. Use Pulse Strike until Mirelume reaches 1 HP. It must not fall below 1.
  4. Use Binding Spore and confirm Snared plus **100% capture chance**.
  5. Use one Aether Prism, continue with Mirelume, open Party, then refresh and open Party again.
- Expected result:
  1. The existing captured party survives refresh, or the uncaptured path visibly identifies both sides and starts with five Prisms.
  2. Mirelume stays at 1 HP, Snare produces 100%, and that Prism captures without another encounter.
  3. Party contains Kivren and Mirelume before and after refresh.
- Pass condition: all applicable expected results occur; a prior capture may make Steps 2–5 BLOCKED rather than failed.
- Fail condition: wrong labels/count, Mirelume reaches 0, a 100% Prism breaks, party placement fails, or refresh loses progress.
- Evidence if failed: one screenshot showing Build 0.0.8 plus the wrong battle/party state and the exact last action.
- Cleanup/reset: close Party on Mossmere Path; do not clear site data.
- Result: PENDING
